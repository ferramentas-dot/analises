# **The First 7-12 Seconds**

How do you cause your prospects to let their guard down within the first few seconds of any sales call, virtual meeting, or in person meeting where they want to open up and engage with you?

P.S. I don’t mean by cracking jokes or asking them how their day is going.

# **PART TWO**

# **PART TWO**

# **Advanced Verbal Cues**

How to bridge from question to question where it feels so natural to the prospect it triggers massive trust in their brain towards you.

This is one of the biggest reasons salespeople lose sales now. Because they don’t know how to bridge from question-to-question - making them sound “scripted” and inauthentic…

Which causes prospects to stay surface level as they feel you are interrogating them. This is one of the BIGGEST reasons why you get multiple objections.

# **PART THREE**

# **PART THREE**

# **Advanced Verbal Pausing**

This is one of the biggest verbal mistakes your company has never taught you… simply because they don’t even know what it means.

It doesn’t mean pausing or creating arbitrary silences… it’s about making the prospect hang onto every word and internalizing questions and consequences… so they feel the internal tension and want to act with urgency.

# **PART FOUR**

# **PART FOUR**

# **Advanced Verbal Pacing**

Have you ever noticed when you ask a long question that your prospects tune out? Or even worse you ask a long question and they just give you a vague, generalized, surface level answer?

If you get the “I want to think it over” objection, or the “I need to do more research objection” or “I need to get more quotes objection or the “I’ll just do this myself” objection, or the “I’m not sure this would work for my industry, my situation, my whatever” objection then that means you haven’t learned yet how to verbally pace where your prospects hang on to every word you use, and every question you ask.

This ONE SKILL alone could double your sales….
